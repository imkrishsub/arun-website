Arun Murugan is an experienced banking operations professional currently employed as Senior Operations Analyst at HDFC Bank in Chennai. Over four years, he has built strong expertise across payment systems (RTGS, NEFT, SWIFT, SEPA), trade finance, account reconciliation, KYC/AML compliance, and core banking platforms including Finacle and SAP.

He is known for high accuracy and a disciplined, process-driven approach. At HDFC Bank, he introduced Excel macro automation that reduced end-of-day reconciliation time by 23% and has maintained a zero-escalation record on AML screening for over fourteen months.
